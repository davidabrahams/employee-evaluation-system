To sort:

1.	Click the column name you would like to sort the employees by
2.	This will sort them in ascending order. If you would like to sort in descending order, click the column again.

The reorder:

1.	Select the employee(s) you would like to shift
2.	Use the up and down arrows to shift the employees.
3.	If an error message appears, an illegal shift may have been performed.
